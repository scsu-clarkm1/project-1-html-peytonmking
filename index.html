Featured Recipe - Ra-Tteokbokki (라볶이)

Ra-Tteokbokki is a Korean rice cake dish with many variations and a rich history. Literally translated as "stir-fried rice cake", is a rice cake dish with fish cake, ramen and egg in a sweet and spicey sauce.

Ingredients Needed

Make sure you have the following on hand before you begin.

  • 1 Bag of Tteok
  • Quart of Cold Water
  • 6 Dashi
  • Handful of Anchovies
  • Quarter of an Onion
  • 2 Tbs of Sugar
  • 1 Tbs of Pepper powder
  • 2 Tbs of Pepper Pasta
  • 2 Tbs of Soysauce
  • 1 Bag of Udon
  • 2 Packets of Ramen

Instructions

  1. Soak frozen tteok is water for a few hours
  2. Add cold water to large pot.
  3. Add dashi, anchovies and onion to pot.
  4. Bring pot to a boil and let boil for 10 minutes with lid on.
  5. Take all solids out of pot, leaving newly created stock.
  6. Add sugar, pepper powder, pepper pasta and soysauce.
  7. Let continue to boil for a few minutes.
  8. Add udon, tteok and ramen until cooked.
  9. Once cooked remove, reduce heat and remove lid.
  10. Let simmer until reduced to your liking.
  11. Serve and enjoy!
